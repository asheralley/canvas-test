//About

Hello, I'm Asher Alley, a front end web developer located in Albany, Auckland. I recently made the switch to web design and development after falling in love with coding, and the process of building a website from original concepts through to unique, user-friendly sites. In May 2020 I graduated with a Diploma in Web and Application Development from the Yoobee School of Design.

Since January 2017 I have been with Veratech Web Solutions as a sub-contractor. I have worked on several projects including developing proprietary software and building an online tutorial web application for the Real Estate Institute of New Zealand, and creating brochure websites for Dancing with Data, Pops Games and Mainly Books.

My primary language is Javascript, and I have experience in HTML, CSS, C#, asp.net/MVC, PHP/Wordpress, Java, and the Software Development Life Cycle. I am constantly improving my skills, and I thoroughly enjoy learning new technologies and staying up to date with current trends. At the moment I am learning to develop in React and Vue.

I am looking for a full-time job in web development that allows me to challenge myself, broaden my knowledge, work in a team of like-minded individuals, and gives me the opportunity to create modern, innovative websites that are easy to use.

Outside of web development I enjoy relaxing with my friends and family, reading, customizing my Linux environment and binge watching the Marvel movies.
